# JCS_SoundProxyAction

Action spawns a new gameobject and play the sound, after the
sound is played the gameobject will be destroyed.


## Variables

<table>
  <tr>
    <td>mAudioClip</td>
    <td>Sound to plays.</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>mSoundSettingType</td>
    <td>Sound settings type.</td>
  </tr>
</table>
